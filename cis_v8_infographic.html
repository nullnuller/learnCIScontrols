CIS Controls Version 8

18 Controls · 153 Safeguards · Implementation Groups IG1–IG3
IG1: Essential Cyber Hygiene IG2: Foundational Enterprise Safeguards IG3: Advanced/Sensitive Environments
IG1: 56 IG2 total: 130 IG3 total: 153
01
Inventory and Control of Enterprise Assets

Actively manage laptops, servers, and devices to ensure only authorized assets can access resources.

02
Inventory and Control of Software Assets

Track, approve, and remove software to reduce attack surface and shadow IT.

03
Data Protection

Classify, handle, encrypt, and securely dispose of data to prevent loss and exposure.

04
Secure Configuration of Assets and Software

Harden configs, remove defaults, and continuously enforce secure baselines.

05
Account Management

Manage identities and privileges; enforce least privilege and timely deprovisioning.

06
Access Control Management

Approve and enforce access to data and systems with MFA and segmentation.

07
Continuous Vulnerability Management

Routinely discover, prioritize, and remediate vulnerabilities across assets.

08
Audit Log Management

Collect, retain, and analyze logs to detect, investigate, and recover.

09
Email and Web Browser Protections

Reduce phishing and browser-based attacks with filtering, isolation, and controls.

10
Malware Defenses

Deploy and maintain anti-malware across endpoints and servers with alerting.

11
Data Recovery

Perform reliable, tested backups and rapid recovery for critical data and systems.

12
Network Infrastructure Management

Secure, standardize, and manage routers, switches, and network services.

13
Network Monitoring and Defense

Detect and respond to anomalies using IDS/IPS, telemetry, and alerting.

14
Security Awareness and Skills Training

Build a culture of security with role-based training and phishing tests.

15
Service Provider Management

Govern third parties: contracts, security requirements, monitoring, and offboarding.

16
Application Software Security

Integrate security into SDLC: design, code, test, and fix vulnerabilities early.

17
Incident Response Management

Prepare, detect, triage, contain, and recover with tested IR plans.

18
Penetration Testing

Validate defenses and improve detection through planned, scoped testing.
